Executive Order 13767 , titled Border Security and Immigration Enforcement Improvements , is an executive order issued by United States President Donald Trump on January 25, 2017. [1] [2] The order directs a wall to be built along the Mexico–United States border . In September 2017, the U.S. federal government announced that prototypes of the wall are under construction. However, the U.S. Congress has not appropriated funds for such a wall to be built, and construction has not begun.
