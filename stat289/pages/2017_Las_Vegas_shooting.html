2017 Las Vegas shooting

The 2017 Las Vegas shooting was a mass shooting on the night of October 1, when a gunman opened fire on a crowd of concertgoers at the Route 91 Harvest music festival on the Las Vegas Strip in Nevada . Perpetrator Stephen Paddock , 64, of Mesquite, Nevada , fired more than 1,100 rounds from his suite on the 32nd floor of the nearby Mandalay Bay hotel, killing 58 people and leaving 851 injured from gunfire and the resulting panic. The shooting occurred between 10:05 and 10:15   p.m. PDT ; about an hour later Paddock was found dead in his room from a self-inflicted gunshot wound. His motive remains unknown.
