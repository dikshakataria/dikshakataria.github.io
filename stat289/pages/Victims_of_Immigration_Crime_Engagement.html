Victims of Immigration Crime Engagement

The Victims of Immigration Crime Engagement ( VOICE ) Office is a U.S. government agency established within the Department of Homeland Security under the Trump administration in February 2017. [1] [2] President Donald Trump directed it be established by Executive Order 13768 . [3]
