The Iraq War ( 2003 to the present), also known as the Second Gulf War is an ongoing war started by the 2003 invasion of Iraq . Following the successful overthrow of the dictator Saddam Hussein , the subsequent occupation of Iraq by a United States -led coalition has resulted in ongoing asymmetric warfare between an insurgency and the occupying coalition forces and sectarian violence between Sunni and Shia Iraqis. [24] The causes and consequences of the war remain controversial. [25] [22] [19]

The Iraq war or war in Iraq Template:Fn , Template:Fn is both an informal and formal term for military conflicts in Iraq that began with the invasion of 2003 by the multinational coalition of American , British , and other forces. Template:Fn The term " Iraq War " has typically referred to the large-scale military hostilities against Saddam Hussein's Iraqi government. This conflict has also been called " The Second Gulf War " and " Gulf War II ".
